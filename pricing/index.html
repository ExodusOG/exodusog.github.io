Requirement for Commercial Use

Any commercial use of content published by Exodus Data requires a paid commercial license.

No commercial rights are granted under the default site license. This requirement applies regardless of how the content is accessed, including via crawling, scraping, indexing, caching, or storage.

What Constitutes Commercial Use

Commercial use includes, without limitation:

  • Use in a commercial product, service, or offering
  • Redistribution or resale of content or derivatives
  • Use by or for a business entity for internal operations or analytics
  • Training, evaluation, or operation of commercial AI or machine learning systems
  • Inclusion in datasets, indexes, or knowledge bases used commercially

License Scope and Pricing

Commercial license terms and pricing depend on the scope of use, volume, distribution model, and intended application.

Exodus Data does not publish standardized pricing tiers. Each commercial license is issued under explicit terms appropriate to the use case.

How to Obtain a Commercial License

To request a paid commercial license, contact Exodus Data with the following information:

  • Organization or individual name
  • Description of intended use
  • Content or datasets involved
  • Estimated volume or scale of use

Contact details are available at /contact/.
